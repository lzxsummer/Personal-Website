Artworks and art news sharing website

functional

Virtual,This is designed for a website which shares daily popular artworks from different fields such as illustration, photography, gaming, movies, blog.

I used a composition of main part and side bar.On the side bar, the webpage will randomly show latiest dynamics.

The design of blocks derived from post cards, I want to give the whole website a friendly and delicated ui that users will have comfortable and familiar feelings when they're using it.

Due to the amount of distrincts and informations to be displayed, I centralized news blocks of different fields in a single div, then used a top right navigation to switch from this field to the other, which made it easy to browse that every field can be gathered in one place.

Theme oriented website

functional

Virtual,This is designed to make a theme website. I didn't find the unique title of it that I used random images here just for presentation.

Those numbers are not only used to connect different blocks of the whole webpage but also made to act as guiding figures to give users a vision journey.

I've been working hard on the diversity of blocks. I used similar block designs on my first webpage, this time I wanted to break the frame and made something stay on one theme but look different and interesting. During this designing experience, I found theme colors with repeating elements are the most important things to make your website stay in one theme. If that is secured, we can make some audacious designs for blocks and compositions.
